Optimum Veranda

The Optimum Veranda is the perfect entry-level solution for those who seek quality without compromise. Crafted from high-grade, 2 mm thick aluminum, it offers a robust and dependable structure that stands the test of time. What sets it apart is the double-reinforced gutter, enhancing both stability and longevity—ideal for long-term use in all weather conditions.

This model is capable of supporting a polycarbonate roof up to 8 meters wide or a glass roof up to 3.5 meters, making it one of the most versatile verandas in its class. Whether you're looking for a sleek look or something more traditional, the Optimum series comes in elegant style

To make installation faster and easier, every Optimum veranda comes pre-fitted with essential components. This includes all necessary rubber seals, integrated LED lighting, and matching screws — so your veranda is nearly ready from the moment it’s delivered.

Reliable, customizable, and smartly engineered, the Optimum series offers everything you need to extend your living space with confidence and style.
