Premium Veranda

Our Premium model is our top-of-the-line veranda, designed for ultimate strength and durability. Crafted from extra-thick 2.5 mm aluminum and reinforced with a double-layer gutter, this model easily withstands the toughest weather conditions—from icy winters to scorching summers, anywhere in the world.

It’s engineered to support polycarbonate roofs of virtually any depth and glass roofs up to 4 meters, giving you maximum flexibility in design and coverage. Just like our Optimum model, the Premium Veranda comes complete with all essential accessories: rubber seals, fasteners, and optional built-in LED lighting for a polished, finished look.

Whether you're upgrading your garden or creating a cozy outdoor room, the Premium Veranda offers elite performance, refined style, and long-term reliability—ideal for homeowners who want only the best.
