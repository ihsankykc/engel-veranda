About Engel Veranda

Crafting outdoor living with purpose and passion

Our Story

Engel Veranda was founded with a single mission: to help people enjoy their outdoor spaces in comfort and style. With a team of skilled designers and installers, we’ve been delivering tailor-made verandas, glass enclosures, and garden rooms across Europe for over a decade.

We believe outdoor living should feel personal, luxurious, and seamless. That’s why every project we undertake is guided by your vision and brought to life with craftsmanship you can trust.

Our Mission

To deliver unique outdoor living spaces that blend functionality and elegance.

Our Vision

To become one of Europe’s most trusted names in verandas and garden room design.
