Glass Sliding Walls

At Engel Veranda, we offer premium glass sliding wall systems that combine high-quality materials with refined aesthetics. Our panels are available in three standard widths — 93 cm, 98 cm, and 103 cm — and heights ranging from 193 cm to 238 cm, with 5 cm increments. Thanks to our adjustable profile system, each panel can be extended up to 12 cm higher, allowing for a maximum installation height of 250 cm.

All our glass panels are made from 10 mm thick tempered safety glass, ensuring both durability and peace of mind. The system’s aluminum profiles are available in two classic RAL colors: RAL 9001 (cream white) and RAL 7016 (anthracite grey).

  • Bottom track with 3, 4, or 5 rails
  • Side profiles
  • Top profile
  • Weather seals
  • Optional handles

Seamless Integration for Your Outdoor Space

Glass sliding doors from Engel Veranda create a weather-protected enclosure for your veranda or patio roof without compromising openness or natural light. They offer a clear, panoramic view of your garden while shielding you from wind and rain — resulting in a bright, spacious environment that can be enjoyed year-round.

Constructed from premium aluminum and clear safety glass, these doors elevate any outdoor structure. When fully enclosed, your veranda is transformed into a stylish garden room — perfect for relaxation in every season.

Our systems are fully custom-made and compatible with most veranda and patio roof brands. They can be installed as front and/or side walls, tailored precisely to your project requirements.

  • Available in HR, HR+, and HR++ configurations
  • Includes tempered safety glass as standard
  • Fully custom-sized to fit your space
  • Elegant, durable, and low-maintenance
